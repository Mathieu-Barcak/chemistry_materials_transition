## What I Learned About Myself as a Learner

My initial plan was to transcribe all handwritten notes at the end of each week, but I found the transcription process longer and more tedious than expected. Spending too much time formatting notes can be distracting from the actual learning. To address this, I will upload handwritten notes immediately and, after three to four weeks, dedicate time to transcribe and review them. This approach will create built-in spaced repetition, separating initial learning from later review and strengthening recall while minimizing distractions during study.
